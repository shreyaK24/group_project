      Kids Fancy Dress

      When you think of kids fancy dress, your mind casts back to the days spent saving the world in your super hero costume or marrying prince charming in your princess outfit. At Smiffys were no different and still think the way to unlock a child's imagination is through fancy dress. Whether it's girls fancy dress, or boys fancy dress, we've got you covered. We carry all the themes you can think of, and some fantastic licensed designs too. We also have a huge range of kids costumes for all the major seasonal events too. Leaving you and your child spoilt for choice on what to wear next. Made to the strictest safety standards, all children's fancy dress complies with EN71 regulations, Nightwear (safety) standards and the European Toy Safety Directive. So you can be sure your little ones stay safe with Smiffys.
